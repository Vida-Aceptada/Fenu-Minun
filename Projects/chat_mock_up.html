Help

Using the chat:

To use the chat simply click on the bottom left text box and type in using your keyboard. You should recieve replies from the chat bot.

Adding a Username:

To change your username click on the "Username" button on the bottom right corner of the chat. Then type in your desired username into the text box and click the save button.

The username length is limited to 1-20 characters.

Customizing Chat Colors:

To customize the chat colors click on the "Colors" button on the bottom right corner of the chat.

You should see different properties that are customizable. You can tweak each one to your liking by clicking on the colored box to the right of each which will bring up a color selection toolbox.

To change each color select or enter the desired color and click "OK". When you are finished click save to apply the changes.
